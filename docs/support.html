Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do I need an account? No. The game is fully playable without signing up. A badge name is only needed if you opt in to the leaderboard or post a scenario idea.
  • How do I get on the leaderboard? Choose a badge name when prompted; your run scores then sync automatically.
  • Can I remove my leaderboard entry or a submitted idea? Yes - use the contact form above with your badge name and we'll remove it.
  • Is my data private? See our Privacy Policy.
