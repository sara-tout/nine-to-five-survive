Privacy Policy

9 to 5: Survive · Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the mobile game 9 to 5: Survive ("the App", "we", "us") collects, how it is used, and your choices. We designed the App to collect as little as possible. The App does not require an account, an email address, or your real name, and it does not show ads or use third-party advertising or tracking technologies.

Information We Collect

  • Badge name (username): A nickname you choose if you opt in to the community leaderboard or post a scenario idea. You decide what this is - it does not need to identify you.
  • Gameplay results: Scores, runs completed, wins, and best streaks, used to power the leaderboard when you choose to sync.
  • Submitted content: Scenario ideas you voluntarily write and submit, and the ideas you "like". Submitted ideas are publicly visible to other players alongside your badge name.
  • Anonymous identifier: A random ID generated on your device and stored locally. It is not linked to your identity, your Apple ID, your device's advertising identifier, or any hardware identifier. It is used only to group diagnostic events from the same installation.
  • Usage and diagnostic data: Anonymous telemetry such as in-app event names, the app version, the platform (e.g. iOS), timestamps, and error/crash details (error messages and stack traces). This helps us find and fix bugs and understand how features are used.

We do not collect your precise location, contacts, photos, microphone or camera data, health data, or your Apple ID. We do not use the App to track you across other companies' apps or websites.

Where Your Data Is Stored

Game progress and the anonymous identifier are stored locally on your device. When you use the community features (leaderboard, scenario ideas) or when diagnostic telemetry is sent, the relevant data is transmitted to and stored by our backend provider, Supabase, which hosts our database on our behalf. Data is transmitted over encrypted (HTTPS) connections.

How We Use the Data

  • To operate community features such as the leaderboard and the scenario idea board.
  • To diagnose crashes, fix bugs, and improve gameplay and stability.
  • To understand which features are used so we can prioritize improvements.

We do not sell your data, and we do not use it for advertising.

Your Choices

  • The App is fully playable offline without submitting any data to our backend.
  • Leaderboard sync and idea posting are optional and only happen when you choose to use them.
  • You can request deletion of leaderboard entries or submitted ideas associated with your badge name by contacting us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
